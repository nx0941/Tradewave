About Tradewave

Building a global ingredient company from India.

Tradewave Ingredients Pvt. Ltd. is being established to connect India's agricultural depth with global food, nutrition and ingredient industries.

Who we are

We are in the inception stage of creating an export-oriented ingredient platform focused on premium dehydrated powders and future value-added plant-based ingredient categories.

Our approach is intentionally transparent: we are developing the business, validating international demand, building technical capability and seeking long-term partnerships with buyers who value quality, consistency and growth.
